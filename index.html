PILLED FITNESS APP PROPOSAL

Proof of Attendance and Fitness Gamification System for Solana Ecosystem

Project Overview

This project will deliver a robust Proof of Attendance and Fitness Tracking Application integrated within the Solana ecosystem. The system will feature:

  • NFC/QR-based in-person event attendance tracking.
  • Gamified fitness challenges and rewards.
  • Integration with Solana ecosystem wallets.
  • Decentralized data storage.
  • Cross-platform compatibility.
  • Scalable infrastructure.
  • User-friendly interface.
  • NFT issuance as immutable participation proof.

    • Project Objectives

  • Enable seamless Proof of Attendance using phone taps, QR codes, or wristbands.
  • Build user engagement via gamification, rewards, and exercise tracking.
  • Foster an NFT-based participation economy within the fitness community.
  • Provide a platform for coaches to monetize their content and workouts.
  • Ensure cross-compatibility with Solana ecosystem devices and applications.

    • Features

    • Proof of Attendance System

      NFC/QR code/wristband scanning to confirm event attendance.

    • Gamified Participation Tracking

      Track attendance, exercise completion, and leaderboard ranking.

    • Expanded Exercise Tracking

      Broaden the range and accuracy of tracked exercises.

    • Device Compatibility

      Solana Phone, Jambo, and other ecosystem-specific devices.

    • NFT Participation Proof

      Issue rare, immutable NFTs as event or achievement proof.

    • Coach Integration Platform

      Subscription model for coaches to create, sell, and share workout plans.

    • Unlockable In-Game Perks

      Allow users to unlock special rewards via achievements.

    • Solana App Integrations

      Connect seamlessly with apps like Moonwalk and other Solana partners.
